# AI Jobs Analytics Dashboard

## Project Description
This project presents an AI Jobs Analytics Dashboard that analyzes global trends in the Artificial Intelligence job market using interactive data visualization. The dashboard explores important factors such as job demand across AI roles, career growth opportunities, work-life balance by country, automation risk, industry investment in AI, and salary distribution. The objective of this project is to transform raw job market data into meaningful insights and help understand how AI adoption is shaping employment trends across industries and countries.

## Project Objectives
- Analyze demand for different AI job roles
- Compare career growth opportunities across countries
- Understand work-life balance trends in the AI workforce
- Examine industry investment in artificial intelligence
- Visualize job openings, salaries, and automation risk

## Tools Used
- Excel (Data preparation and cleaning)
- Tableau (Dashboard creation and data visualization)

## Dashboard Components

### KPI Indicators
The dashboard includes key metrics that summarize the AI job market:
- AI Automation Score
- Career Growth Score
- Work-Life Balance Score
- Automation Risk
- Average AI Experience Required

### Promotion Level Analysis
This visualization compares salary percentile levels across AI job roles including AI Engineer, Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, NLP Engineer, and Research Scientist.

### Work-Life Balance by Country
A bubble chart shows work-life balance scores across multiple countries such as India, USA, UK, Germany, Japan, Canada, and Australia.

### Career Growth by Country
A stacked area chart visualizes how AI roles are growing across different countries and markets.

### Industry Funding
A pie chart illustrates AI investment distribution across industries including Finance, Healthcare, Retail, Automotive, Telecom, and Education.

### Job Openings by Role
A bar chart compares the number of job openings across various AI-related roles.

### Layoff Risk Analysis
This chart evaluates the probability of layoffs across different AI careers.

### Average Salary and Bonus
A visualization comparing salary distribution across AI roles.

## Key Insights
- AI job demand is increasing globally.
- Machine Learning Engineers and AI Engineers show the highest demand.
- Countries with strong technology ecosystems provide better career growth opportunities.
- Industries such as finance and healthcare are investing heavily in AI technologies.
- AI careers offer competitive salaries and relatively stable employment.

## Conclusion
The AI Jobs Analytics Dashboard provides valuable insights into how artificial intelligence is transforming the global job market. The project highlights the growing demand for AI professionals and demonstrates how data visualization can be used to analyze complex datasets and communicate insights effectively.
